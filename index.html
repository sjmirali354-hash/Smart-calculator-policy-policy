OFFLINE USAGE

The core calculator, scientific calculator, and unit conversion features work fully offline.

Currency exchange rates require an internet connection. Once downloaded, the rates are cached on your device for up to 6 hours, allowing the converter to work without a connection during that period.

NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED

DATA DELETION

To delete all app data, simply uninstall the application. This permanently removes all locally stored history, cached exchange rates, and settings from your device.
